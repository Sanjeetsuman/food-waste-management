About Us

  • Once upon a time, there was a small town where food waste was a big problem. Every day, tons of perfectly good food was thrown away, causing environmental harm and social injustice. But one day, a group of concerned citizens decided to take action.
  • They formed a food waste management project with the goal of reducing food waste and redistributing surplus food to those in need. They started by partnering with local grocery stores, restaurants, and farmers markets to collect their excess food. Instead of throwing it away, they distributed the food to local food banks and shelters.
